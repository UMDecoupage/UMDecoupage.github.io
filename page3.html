Unique Metal Decoupage

Artist Catherine Kill

Catherine is and artist who lives in West Penwith, Cornwall. She has lived in this area with her husband and two daughters for 30 years.
Over this time she has worked with diffrent media, producing paintings, pottery, pyrographic art and pieces from recycled glass. Continuing the theme of recycling the pictures you see here today are all made from discarded aluminium cans.
After flattening the metal, the images are hand formed and painted using special inks.
Each picture is unique and there are no prints made of them. Much of the her art is accepted on a commission basis.
Her art can be seen from time to time in various  galleries in the region.
